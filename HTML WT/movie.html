MOVIE REVIEW

BHEEMLA NAYAK

Story and Remake

Bheemla Nayak is a 2022 Indian Telugu-language action thriller film directed by Saagar K Chandra, based on the 2020 Malayalam film Ayyappanum Koshiyum. The screenplay was adapted by Trivikram Srinivas, with dialogues by Trivikram Srinivas and story by Sachy.

Cast

The film stars Pawan Kalyan and Rana Daggubati in lead roles.

Plot

The story revolves around an upright police officer, Bheemla Nayak (Pawan Kalyan), and a retired army officer, Daniel Shekar (Rana Daggubati), whose egos clash over a petty case. As their quarrel intensifies, they engage in a series of intense confrontations.

Music

The film features music composed by Thaman S.
